About Me

I am a Machine Learning Engineer and Data Scientist with hands-on experience designing, training, and deploying production-grade AI solutions. I enjoy building systems that don’t just look smart on paper, but are actually used in the wild—shaping decisions, automating workflows, and unlocking new ways for teams to work with data.

At AXA Mansard, I work at the intersection of data engineering, applied machine learning, and MLOps. I design predictive models, develop ETL pipelines, containerise and deploy microservices with FastAPI and Docker, and collaborate with business teams to ship solutions that make sense in the real world—not just in a Jupyter notebook.

Outside of work, I experiment with OpenVINO-based computer vision, NLP systems, and end-to-end ML pipelines. I’ve participated in data science competitions and hackathons, contributed to open-source-style projects and reference implementations, and continue to explore how AI can be both powerful and responsible.
